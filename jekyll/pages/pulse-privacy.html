PULSE - Close Your Eyes Privacy Policy

privacy@pulse:~$ cat policy.txt

Effective date: June 29, 2026

This privacy policy applies to PULSE - Close Your Eyes for iOS.

Information the app collects

PULSE - Close Your Eyes does not collect personal data. It does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, third-party SDKs, or a developer-operated server.

Information stored on your device

The app stores gameplay progress and preferences on your device. This includes things like sound settings, win and loss counts, best times, streaks, and palette progress.

This information is used only to run the game and show your local progress. It is not sent to me. If your device backup includes app data, that backup is handled by Apple or your backup provider, not by PULSE - Close Your Eyes.

Game Center

If you use multiplayer, the app uses Apple Game Center for sign-in, matchmaking, and communication between matched players. Apple handles Game Center account data under Apple's Privacy Policy.

During a match, the app exchanges gameplay messages with the matched opponent through GameKit, including race start, touch position, result, and disconnect events. PULSE - Close Your Eyes does not operate a server for this and does not store those messages after the match.

Device permissions

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, photos, contacts, or tracking permission. It uses haptics and audio output locally on your device.

Children

PULSE - Close Your Eyes does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, I will update this page and the effective date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to forketyfork@icloud.com.
