PULSE - Close Your Eyes Support

support@pulse:~$ cat help.txt

If you need help with PULSE - Close Your Eyes, email forketyfork@icloud.com.

What to include

Please include your iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened.

Device support

PULSE - Close Your Eyes needs precise vibration feedback to play. Please use an iPhone with vibration feedback; iPads and older iPhones are not supported.
